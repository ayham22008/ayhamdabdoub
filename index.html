About Me

A Computer Graphics and Animation graduate passionate about creating creative digital experiences through design, technology, and interactive media.

Who I Am

My name is Ayham Nidal Dabdoub. I am a graduate of Al-Balqa Applied University (BAU), Prince Abdullah Bin Ghazi Faculty of ICT, with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Graphics and Animation.

During my university journey, I developed experience in graphic design, UI/UX design, 3D modeling, animation, and game development. I worked on different creative and technical projects that helped me combine artistic vision with programming and interactive technologies.

My graduation project, "War Of Knowledge", is an educational strategy game developed using Unity Engine and C#. The project combines storytelling, game mechanics, user interface design, and educational content.

I also have experience working as a team leader and project manager, where I developed strong skills in teamwork, communication, problem solving, time management, and organizing creative projects from idea to final result.
